Property Owner Help Guide

Welcome to your complete workflow guide for managing your properties on Yellowkey.

Quick Overview

As a Property Owner, you can register your profile, list properties for rent or sale, view property status, manage inquiries, and communicate with potential tenants or buyers.

Property Owner Workflow
1
Register and Create Profile
Create your account, complete your profile with contact details, and verify your identity to start listing properties.
2
List a Property
Add your property with complete details, photos, pricing, and choose whether to list yourself or assign an agent.
3
View Property Status
Monitor your property's visibility, views, inquiries, and current status (Available, Rented, Under Maintenance).
4
Manage Inquiries
Review inquiries from interested tenants or buyers. Respond to questions and schedule property viewings.
5
Communicate with Agents
If you've assigned an agent, stay in touch to track progress, approve viewings, and finalize rental/sale agreements.

Detailed Instructions

  • Click "Sign Up" or "Register" on the homepage
  • Choose "Property Owner" account type
  • Fill in registration details:
    • Full name
    • Email address
    • Phone number
    • Password (secure)
  • Verify your email address
  • Complete your profile:
    • Upload ID/QID (for verification)
    • Add alternative contact numbers
    • Set communication preferences
  • Accept terms and conditions
  • Click "Complete Registration"

  • Log in to your dashboard
  • Click "Add New Property" or "List Property"
  • Choose listing type:
    • Direct Listing: You manage inquiries yourself
    • Agent Listing: Assign a Yellowkey agent to handle it
  • Fill in property details:
    • Property type (Villa, Apartment, Office, etc.)
    • Location (Zone, Street name, Building)
    • Size (square meters)
    • Bedrooms, bathrooms, parking
    • Amenities (gym, pool, security, etc.)
    • Rental/Sale price
    • Availability date
  • Upload photos (minimum 5, maximum 20)
  • Write property description
  • Set viewing preferences
  • Review and publish listing

  • Navigate to "My Properties" in your dashboard
  • View all your listed properties
  • Check property metrics:
    • Total views
    • Number of inquiries
    • Days on market
    • Current status
  • Property statuses explained:
    • Active: Visible to potential tenants/buyers
    • Under Negotiation: In discussion with a client
    • Rented/Sold: Deal completed
    • Under Maintenance: Temporarily unavailable
    • Inactive: Hidden from search results
  • Update property status as needed
  • Edit property details anytime

  • Access "Inquiries" section from dashboard
  • View inquiries organized by property
  • Each inquiry shows:
    • Client name and contact details
    • Inquiry date
    • Client's message/questions
    • Viewing preferences
    • Budget range
  • Respond to inquiries:
    • Answer questions professionally
    • Schedule viewings
    • Negotiate terms if needed
  • Mark inquiries as:
    • Active (in discussion)
    • Viewing scheduled
    • Converted (successful rental/sale)
    • Closed (no longer interested)

  • If you assigned an agent to your property:
    • View agent profile and contact details
    • Access direct messaging with your agent
    • Receive updates on viewings and inquiries
  • Communication best practices:
    • Respond to agent messages promptly
    • Approve or decline viewing requests quickly
    • Discuss pricing and terms openly
    • Review agent performance regularly
  • Monitor agent activity:
    • Number of viewings scheduled
    • Inquiries handled
    • Marketing efforts
  • Approve final rental/sale agreements
  • Coordinate handover with agent

Tips & Best Practices

  • Complete Profile: Fill all profile details to build trust with potential tenants.
  • Quality Photos: Upload clear, well-lit photos showing all rooms and amenities.
  • Accurate Information: Provide correct property details to avoid wasting time.
  • Competitive Pricing: Research market rates for similar properties in your area.
  • Quick Responses: Respond to inquiries within 24 hours to secure quality tenants.
  • Regular Updates: Keep property information current (availability, pricing, photos).
  • Professional Descriptions: Write clear, honest property descriptions.
  • Flexible Viewing: Offer multiple viewing time slots to accommodate clients.
  • Verify Tenants: Request proper identification and references.
  • Use Agents Wisely: Consider agent services for premium properties or if you're busy.
